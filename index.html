Next Best Offer Recommender System

Abstract: Developed a customer-centric recommendation platform for a major bank, adapting Andrea Fabrizi’s (2014) Next Best Offer framework. Since real customer data was unavailable due to NDA, synthetic data was generated to validate the approach.

Role: Designed and implemented recommendation pipelines, built propensity models, and created synthetic datasets for testing.

Tech Stack: Python, PyTorch, Pandas, Scikit-learn.

Impact: Enabled accurate product recommendations in an environment with limited data, supporting customer engagement strategies.
